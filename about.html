About Me

I’m a mathematician, data analyst, and builder. During my degree, I focused on advanced mathematics, problem solving, and helping others learn. I took a nontraditional route, made a lot of mistakes, and came out stronger for it.

Right now, I build tools, dashboards, and systems — anything that helps people work better. I’m obsessed with understanding things at a deep level and don’t accept fake clarity. I like to work on projects that connect real people to meaningful solutions.

Education

B.S. in Mathematics from Florida International University

Courses Taken

  • Calculus I, II, III: Taught me to understand the world in a physical sense.
  • Linear Algebra: Pattern recognition for systems of objects.
  • Abstract Algebra: More pattern recognition, but abstract and fancy.
  • Real Analysis: Taught me how to be a pedantic pain in the ass and dive deep.
  • Complex Analysis: Showed me how to think abstractly.
  • Differential Equations: Motion, motion, motion.
  • Topology: Generality of spaces and what makes them unique.
  • Partial Differential Equations: That ODEs live inside PDEs, and we can deconstruct complex motions into solvable pieces.
  • Mathematical Modeling: Turning messy reality into something math pretends to handle.
  • Probability & Statistics: Taught me how to love myself—because it was when I hated myself most.
  • Machine Learning: Showed me the craziness of AI and that these systems need deep refining.
